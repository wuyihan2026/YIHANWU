/May, 2025

Garment, Image: Yihan Wu

Derived fromSelf-Healing,Pressextends the investigation of body and structure through image. The series consists of scanned garment details produced using a professional print scanner.

The images isolate textures, seams, and structural fragments, shifting focus from silhouette to material presence. In several images, garments are scanned through transparent protective covers, introducing an additional layer between surface and image. This mediating layer compresses and distorts the material, producing subtle tensions between clarity and obstruction.

Approaching scanning as a process of pressure and examination, the work flattens and recontextualizes the garments, revealing underlying structures and variations, where rotation, deformation, and repetition become perceptible across the series.
