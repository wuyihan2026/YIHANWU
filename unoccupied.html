Unoccupied
/2025-2026

Photography:YIHAN WU

Unoccupiedobserves chairs as part of everyday spatial situations, focusing on moments where their function appears paused or suspended.

Rather than treating them as isolated objects, the work considers the conditions they inhabit — scenes that feel incomplete, as if something has just happened or is about to occur. These spaces carry traces of use without fully revealing them, producing a quiet sense of anticipation.

Shot on iPhone, the images are captured intuitively while moving through space, without staging or intervention. The project forms an open-ended archive, reflecting recurring patterns of use, order, and temporality embedded in ordinary environments.
