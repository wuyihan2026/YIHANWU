Self-healing
/April, 2025

Fashion design & Art direction: Yihan Wu
Photography: Rino Qiu
Make up & Hair: Yujia Liu
Talent: Alban Shajaku

Self-Healing explores the body as a site of transformation, shaped by both physical condition and lived experience. Developed from her personal experience with scoliosis, the project reflects on imbalance not as a flaw to be corrected, but as a structural and aesthetic potential.

Asymmetry becomes the central language of the work. Displaced and rotated pattern pieces generate tilted constructions that appear unstable in their flat state, yet form dynamic, twisted silhouettes when worn — responding directly to the movement of the body.

Informed by research into historical scoliosis treatments, particularly the principle of counter-directional pulling, the garments translate tension, rotation, and release into form. This approach is further developed through movement-based experiments, where elastic resistance and bodily extension inform softer, more adaptive structures.

Moving away from rigid and corrective systems, Self-Healing proposes a more fluid and self-directed relationship with the body — where imbalance is not resolved, but continuously negotiated.
