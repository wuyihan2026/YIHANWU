'Not Yet'
/March, 2026

Creative Direction, Styling: Yihan Wu
Photography: Eloise Cao
Make up & Hair: Zhaojun Han
Talent: Xiwei Chen

Not Yetexplores the moment before an event occurs, focusing on conditions where risk is anticipated but never realized.

Set within seemingly stable environments, the images reveal subtle misalignments between body, object, and space — small spatial tensions that introduce the possibility of danger without leading to it. These conditions remain suspended, inviting projection rather than resolution.

Through staged scenes of everyday actions — eating, cleaning, grooming, ironing — the work constructs situations in which something appears about to happen. The character remains unaware, while the viewer gradually perceives these latent risks through composition and object placement.

Using fashion imagery as a visual framework, clothing, posture, and styling organise the relationship between body and environment. Within this controlled system, tension is deliberately maintained, holding each image in a state of anticipation — not yet.
